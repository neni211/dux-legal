🦆 Dux Marketplace - Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Flux Credit Purchases

Flux credits purchased through our payment provider are generally non-refundable once added to your account. However, if you experience a technical issue that prevented you from receiving your Flux credits after payment, please contact us and we will investigate and resolve the issue.

2. Transaction Disputes

If you have purchased a product through Dux Marketplace and have not received it or it does not match the description, you may open a dispute within 1 hour of purchase using the "Sorun Var" (Issue) button in your direct messages from the Dux bot.

3. Escrow Protection

All transactions are protected by our escrow system. Flux credits are held securely and are only released to the seller after:

  • The buyer confirms receipt of the product, or
  • 1 hour passes without a dispute being raised

4. Refund Process

If a dispute is resolved in the buyer's favor, Flux credits will be returned to the buyer's account. Refunds are processed in Flux credits, not in real currency.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • The buyer confirmed receipt of the product
  • 1 hour has passed and no dispute was raised
  • The dispute was resolved in the seller's favor
  • Abuse of the dispute system was detected

6. Contact

For refund-related inquiries, contact us through our Discord support server.
