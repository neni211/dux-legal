🦆 Dux Marketplace - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Dux Marketplace:

  • Discord User ID and username
  • Transaction history within the platform
  • Flux credit balance and purchase history
  • Product listings and store information (for sellers)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain the Dux marketplace
  • Process Flux credit purchases and transactions
  • Resolve disputes between buyers and sellers
  • Prevent fraud and abuse of the platform
  • Improve our services

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Discord: For user authentication and bot interactions
  • Paddle: For processing Flux credit purchases
  • Supabase: For secure data storage

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data with payment processors solely for the purpose of completing transactions.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us on our Discord support server.

7. Cookies

Our web panel may use cookies for authentication purposes only. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Dux Marketplace is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us through our Discord support server.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through our Discord server.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us on our Discord support server.
